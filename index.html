About us

Crinkle Cut Games is a Danish game studio founded by three game development graduates in 2021 after receiving a cultural grant from the Danish Film Institute to build our debut game Discounty. Later, British publisher PQube invested in the project to amplify the scope and reach of Discounty.

Our goal is to build a sustainable and inclusive game studio, focusing on making peaceful and quirky games with well-crafted interactions and a dash of satire and subversion.

We love odd and under-explored genres, especially those many wouldn't consider being "real games" and we are committed to making games for all, regardless of ability, race, or gender expression.
